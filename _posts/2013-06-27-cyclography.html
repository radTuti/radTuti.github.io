I recently just got my hand on the Toronto Cycling Map for 2013. So I am looking through at in and in the Downtown Core Map, I noticed yellow highlighting on certain paths with the legend stating Downtown Explorer Route.

Been living downtown for a few months now, I've gotten bored of my usual routine and I am hoping to start new ones. Looking at the map got me thinking, what if I could bike around the city using the map and capture some pictures along the way? So how is this beneficial to me?

  1. Physical: I get to clock in more hours of physical activity so I don't feel guilty about not doing yoga in the morning or going to the gym in the evening or not signing up for bootcamp classes.
  2. Mental: I get to explore new place and see Toronto different, I call this cyclography [my word Update: initially thought i coined the word]. While exploring the city, I am hoping to learn about the different neighbourhoods and history this city has to offer.
  3. Blog: I get to share my journey through the with the amazing people that read my blog (you!)
  4. Skills: I get to improve my photography skills because I plan to shoot mainly in manual mode. I also want to improve my time spent biking.
  5. I get to #unplug (click link for more details) ...and many more

I am planning on starting this next week. In each post, I will state what route I took and also where pictures are taken. I had been musing over this idea since Tuesday but I finally got inspired by this video to post it on here.

Hopefully I can inspire someone through this project to explore & see Toronto (or any city) differently!
