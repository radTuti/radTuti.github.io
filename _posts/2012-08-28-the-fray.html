It takes wanting something really bad to actually work hard to get it. When you have put in all that you can, let the rest take care of itself or not.

Perfectionism paralyses and prevent one from starting and/ or finishing anything. Also there is data paralysis which overwhelms. Finally, the TED Talk that says when you have an idea, don't say it till you accomplish it because telling people makes you less likely to do it.

When there's so many things against succeeding, one can only wonder why people still bother trying. Humans! You can never fully understand them!
